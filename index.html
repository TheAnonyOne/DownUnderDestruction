About the Game

Take control of Dave, a no-nonsense bricklayer thrust into a battle against the corrupt mega-corporation OzTron. With a demonic brick in hand, face off against waves of enemies across Aussie-inspired levels packed with action, comedy, and chaotic combat.

Features

  • Brick throwing and recall combat
  • Combo-based ability system
  • Iconic Aussie environments
  • Low-poly stylized visuals with modern VFX
  • Story-driven single-player campaign
